Getting Started

The Background Remover uses advanced AI models to automatically detect and remove backgrounds from your images, leaving only the main subject.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Select AI Model: Choose between U²-Net Standard (faster) or ISNet Enhanced (higher quality with alpha matting).
  2. Upload Image: Click "Choose File" or drag and drop your image. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP, BMP, TIFF (max 50MB).
  3. Processing: Wait while the AI processes your image. This typically takes 3-10 seconds depending on the model and image size.
  4. Download Result: Once complete, preview the result and download your image with transparent background.

Common Issues

Upload Failed

Possible causes:

  • File size exceeds 50MB
  • Unsupported file format
  • Network connection issues

Solution: Compress your image or convert to a supported format.

Processing Timeout

Possible causes:

  • Very large image size
  • Server is busy

Solution: Try resizing your image or use U²-Net Standard model for faster processing.

Poor Result Quality

Possible causes:

  • Low image resolution
  • Poor contrast between subject and background
  • Complex background

Solution: Use ISNet Enhanced model and ensure good lighting in your original image.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Images are processed on our servers and are not stored permanently. They are automatically deleted after processing.

Can I process multiple images at once?

Currently, images must be processed one at a time. Batch processing is planned for a future update.

What's the difference between the two AI models?

U²-Net Standard is faster and suitable for most images. ISNet Enhanced uses advanced alpha matting for superior edge quality, especially for fine details like hair, fur, or transparent objects.

Is there a limit on how many images I can process?

Currently, there are no limits. However, please use the service responsibly.
